# Follow the agent lifecycle

Use this page to **design, build and operate agents safely**.

Agents must be controlled at every stage of delivery.

---

# What you must do

You must:

- ✅ Define the use case before building  
- ✅ Apply controls early  
- ✅ Test before deployment  
- ✅ Monitor continuously  

---

# Lifecycle stages

## Discovery

Define the problem and decide if AI is needed.

You must:

- ✅ Define the outcome and user need  
- ✅ Check non-AI alternatives  
- ✅ Identify risks and data requirements  
- ✅ Confirm if an agent is appropriate  

---

## Alpha / Beta

Design and test the solution.

You must:

- ✅ Build prototypes  
- ✅ Define autonomy and controls  
- ✅ Test for:
  - Prompt injection  
  - Tool misuse  
  - Data risks  

- ✅ Create an evaluation plan  
- ✅ Draft DPIA if required  

---

## Live

Deploy and operate the agent.

You must:

- ✅ Monitor performance  
- ✅ Log all interactions  
- ✅ Apply incident response  
- ✅ Maintain human oversight  

---

## Retirement

Decommission safely.

You must:

- ✅ Remove access and integrations  
- ✅ Delete or archive data  
- ✅ Update records and registers  

---

# Apply maturity levels

Use maturity to decide how much control is needed.

---

## Experimenting

Small pilots and proofs of concept.

You should:

- ✅ Keep scope narrow  
- ✅ Use simple patterns  
- ✅ Limit autonomy (read-only or drafting)  

---

## Formalised

Repeatable and governed delivery.

You must:

- ✅ Apply consistent controls  
- ✅ Use logging and monitoring  
- ✅ Define ownership and support  

---

## Scale

Enterprise capability across Defra.

You must:

- ✅ Apply strong governance  
- ✅ Monitor across systems  
- ✅ Reuse components and patterns  

---

# Apply control gates

You must pass these checks before progressing:

- ✅ Clear outcome  
- ✅ Defined user journey  
- ✅ Autonomy and controls defined  
- ✅ Security and data risks assessed  
- ✅ Evaluation completed  

---

# Plan for failure

You must assume agents will:

- Misinterpret instructions  
- Be manipulated (prompt injection)  
- Act incorrectly  

---

✅ You must implement:

- Human override  
- Monitoring and alerts  
- Safe failure modes  

---

# Do

- ✅ Start small  
- ✅ Apply controls early  
- ✅ Test thoroughly  
- ✅ Monitor continuously  

---

# Do not

- ❌ Skip evaluation  
- ❌ Deploy without controls  
- ❌ Assume agents behave correctly  

---

# Summary

You must:

1. Define the problem  
2. Build safely  
3. Test before deployment  
4. Monitor continuously  

> Agents must be safe at every stage of the lifecycle.
