# Follow core principles

All agentic AI systems in Defra must follow these principles.

---

# 1. Keep humans in control

Agents must support decisions, not make them.

You must:

- ✅ Define human approval points  
- ✅ Enable override and escalation  
- ✅ Record decision ownership  

---

# 2. Augment people, not replace them

Agents must improve outcomes.

You must:

- ✅ Measure productivity gains  
- ✅ Maintain expert judgement  
- ✅ Avoid removing critical roles  

---

# 3. Use the simplest solution

You must:

- ✅ Start with existing tools  
- ✅ Avoid unnecessary complexity  
- ✅ Justify custom builds  

---

# 4. Evaluate continuously

Agents must be tested and monitored.

You must:

- ✅ Evaluate before deployment  
- ✅ Monitor after deployment  
- ✅ measure performance and risk  

---

# 5. Design for safety and transparency

You must:

- ✅ Log all actions  
- ✅ Maintain audit trails  
- ✅ Provide explanations  

---

# 6. Control access and data

You must:

- ✅ Minimise data usage  
- ✅ Apply least privilege  
- ✅ Protect sensitive data  

---

# 7. Keep systems understandable

You must:

- ✅ Document behaviour  
- ✅ Make outputs explainable  
- ✅ Allow safe testing  

---

# Do

- ✅ Design for public value  
- ✅ Keep systems observable  
- ✅ Apply controls early  

---

# Do not

- ❌ Remove human accountability  
- ❌ Use AI without evaluation  
- ❌ Allow uncontrolled behaviour  

---

> Human judgement must remain central to all agent systems.
