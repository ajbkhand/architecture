# Agent ↔ Human standards

Human oversight is mandatory.

## Requirements

- Human approval for consequential actions  
- Clear points of intervention  
- Ability to override or stop the agent  

## Audit

Capture:

- Decisions made  
- Approvals given  
- Overrides and reasons  

## UX requirements

- Users must understand:
  - What the agent is doing  
  - Why decisions are made  
  - When they are responsible  

## Principle

Human remains in command at all times.
